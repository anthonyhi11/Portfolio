Projects

Bon Iver Quiz App

The goal of this application is to have a user be tested on their knowledge of my favorite band, Bon Iver. As the user progresses through the quiz, they are presented with a question and 4 options. Once an option is selected and submitted, they are shown if they are correct or incorrect. If incorrect, they can show the correct answer or continue on to the next question. The score is tallied up at the end and they are shown either a congratulations screen or a try again screen depending on the score. The user can then take the quiz again, if they choose.
